Features

Everything you need to earn more.

Six powerful tools working together to maximize every dollar you spend.

Smart Pay

At every checkout, PointsVantage detects the merchant category via GPS and Google Places, then instantly ranks your linked cards by earning rate — so you always tap the highest-multiplier card.

Up to 5x more points per purchase

Card Dashboard

All your balances, offers, and earning history unified in one view. Linked via Plaid — always current, always accurate.

Card Recommender

Upload your spending history and our AI calculates which new card would earn you the most rewards — before you apply.

Max — AI Assistant

Your personal points advisor, powered by Claude. Ask Max anything: which card to use, when to transfer, which redemption gives the best value.

Travel Finder

Search 32+ airline and hotel transfer partners and find the path that stretches your points furthest for any destination.

News & Shorts

Real-time alerts on devaluations, new offers, and limited-time bonuses. Plus a vertical video feed from top points creators.

Process

Up and running in minutes.

01

Link Your Cards

Securely connect your credit cards via Plaid. PointsVantage imports your spending history and point balances automatically — read-only, never transactional.

02

Pay Smarter

Open Smart Pay at any merchant. The app detects where you are and ranks your cards by earning rate in real time. Tap the winner.

03

Redeem Brilliantly

Use Travel Finder and Max AI to route your points through the best transfer partner for your next trip — and never leave value on the table.
